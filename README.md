## Adidas US Sales Performance Analysis (2020 - 2021)
### 1. Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Adidas sales data across the United States. Using a dataset of over 9,637 records, I developed an interactive executive dashboard to track revenue growth, profitability by product line, and regional market share.

Goal: To identify high-margin product categories and optimize sales channel strategies.

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Timeline, Dynamic Labeling).

### 2. Key Business Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks four primary metrics to evaluate business health:

Total Revenue: $120M, representing a 300% growth trend between 2020 and 2021.

Operating Margin: 27.63%, reflecting efficient cost management and pricing strategy.

Average Price Point: $45.22, indicating a consistent mid-to-high-tier product positioning.

Units Sold: 2.4M+ items moved across all sales channels.

### 3. Deep Dive Insights
Product Performance: Men's Street Footwear emerged as the top-performing category, generating the highest operating profit ($8.2M+).

Regional Dominance: The West Region led the market with $36.3M in total revenue, followed closely by the Northeast ($25M).

Channel Efficiency: Online sales moved the highest volume of units (939k), while In-store channels contributed the most to high-value transactions.

Seasonality: Data revealed a significant sales explosion starting in July and August, highlighting a strong "Back-to-School" or mid-year peak.

### 4. Technical Implementation
To ensure a professional and interactive experience, I implemented:

Dynamic Labeling: Used custom number formatting ($#,, "M") to simplify large values for executive-level readability.

Calculated Fields: Built custom formulas within Pivot Tables to accurately measure Operating Margin % without distorting aggregate data.

Interactivity: Integrated Slicers (Region, Product) and Timelines to allow users to filter data dynamically.

Design: Applied a monochromatic "Adidas-branded" dark theme to ensure visual consistency and professional aesthetics.

### 5. Conclusion & Recommendations
Based on the data, I recommend:

Inventory Expansion: Increase stock levels for Street Footwear in the West region during Q3 to capitalize on the July-August peak.

Digital Focus: Further invest in the Online sales channel, as it demonstrates the highest scalability in terms of units sold.
